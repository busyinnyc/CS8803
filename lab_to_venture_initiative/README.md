# CS8803 Mid-Semester Report: Lab-to-Venture (L2V) Initiative

**Author:** Amr Arafa

## Overview
This repository contains the Mid-Semester Report and Operations Reflection for the **Lab-to-Venture (L2V) Framework**. 

The L2V initiative proposes a structured "train-the-trainer" commercialization pipeline. It is designed to empower researchers to translate their academic work into commercial pitch decks, identify industry partners, and sustain long-term external relationships independently, thereby removing top-heavy administrative bottlenecks.

## Key Objectives of the L2V Framework
1. **Decentralize Partnerships:** Shift the burden of managing external logistics from the administrative team directly to the researchers to prevent relationship decay during semester turnovers.
2. **Utilize Existing Networks:** Build a structured "on-ramp" for researchers to seamlessly bring their own professional industry networks into the research group.
3. **Commercial Translation:** Train researchers to pivot from traditional "academic presenting" to delivering concise, commercially viable narratives (Bottom Line Up Front).
